Sidebar (A) — trade-offs

  • More vertical space for charts
  • Familiar for data/monitoring apps
  • Easier to add nav items later
  • Mode badge + timestamp always visible

Top Nav (B) — trade-offs

  • Maximum full-width content area
  • Cleaner on narrow screens
  • 5 tabs is pushing top-nav limits
  • Less room for contextual info (mode, time)

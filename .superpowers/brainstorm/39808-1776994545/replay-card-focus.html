Event Replay — Summary Card Priority

Each crisis window gets a summary card at the top. What's the hero metric?

A

Warning Lead Time

Make the lead-time callout the biggest number on the card. "33 days early" displayed large, with recall, false alerts, and max risk shown small beneath it.

Shows the model's forward-looking value first. Most compelling for a portfolio piece.

B

Max Transition Risk

Peak risk reached during the window is the hero stat. Lead time, regime coverage, and alert count shown below. Useful even when there was no early warning.

Always available — doesn't require an early-warning signal. Works for all 3 windows.

C

Two hero stats side by side

Lead time + max risk shown equally large. Additional stats (alert days, regime breakdown) in a second row beneath them.

More information-dense. Slightly busier but gives a fuller picture at a glance.

Supporting stats shown on all cards

📊 Max transition risk during window
⏱ First threshold crossing date
🔔 Alert days (risk > 0.10)
🎯 Regime match rate (actual vs predicted)
📅 Days in elevated + turbulent regimes
⚡ Warning lead time (if detected)
